Pandemic!

  • Originality: The originality of my game is how I use the Corona Virus Pandemic as the feature of my game. I have not seen a game similar to this yet. The player simply infects others to dominate the world.
  • Technical Merit: What is interesting about my game technology is that I got the non-infected enimies to follow the player sprite and also bounce randomly throughout the game. I also messed around with the Random Generator function to drop in the non-infected players at random places on the map. Moreover, I also figured out how to give all of the sprites "health" so that you dont just infect everyone with a single sneeze, it takes at least three of them.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype? I learned alot from this prototype. I learned how to add health or hp to sprites. I also learned how the "follow tool" works. This was really interesting in seeing how the math works. I also learned how to create a custom theme through this prototype. I dont think anything like this is currently in the game market.
